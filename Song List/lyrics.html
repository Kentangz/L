Song Details

blue

"Blue" by Yung Kai is a dreamy and heartfelt exploration of young love and longing. The song uses vivid imagery to depict romantic moments, such as gazing into a lover's eyes compared to watching stars, and imagining shared experiences under moonlit skies.
